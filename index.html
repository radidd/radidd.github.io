About me

I'm a PhD candidate at the Centre for Doctoral Training in Natural Language Processing at the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Dr Alexandra Birch, Prof Frank Keller and Dr Annie Holtz. My research interests include language production, event description and pragmatics.

Previously I completed the MSc Speech and Language Processing programme and worked as a research assistant with the StatMT group at the University of Edinburgh.
